Under the hood

Green Space Business Manager

Green Space is designed for service-based industries like lawn care
companies, landscapers, and contractors. It allows you to track sales leads
and appointments, schedule jobs and route workers, manage employee payroll,
and track materials used on each job.
Green Space gives you a clear picture of where your business stands in real time so you can make informed decisions on improving your bottom line.

AI Powered Automatic Routing

We’re all about saving you precious time. When it comes to making routes, we
want to say goodbye to manual map routing forever.

It works like this. First, you select your preferred strategy: Most priority
customers or Fastest drive time. Next, we use our AI Route Generator to create
an unlimited number efficient routes that you can choose from and assign to
your team members. And once you approve your routes - voilà! We are off to
work in the most efficient way possible!

Automatic Emails

Automatic emails are a great way to increase sales and to automatically
remind your customers of upcoming services or invoices. It's easy to set up,
and it works continually with no extra effort on your part. Past due invoice
alerts will help with ensuring payments are on time so you have the money
you need to pay bills and run your business smoothly. Studies show unpaid
invoices can be greatly reduced by using automatic payment reminder emails!

Property Measure Tool

Obtain accurate property measurements without stepping foot on
a property with our Property Measure Tool. This easy-to-use free tool uses
satellite imagery to calculate areas down to the square foot. This is all done without having to click and drop any points on the map at all. It is completely automated! Now you won’t have to worry about training new employees on how to measure a property and you can always ensure the measurement is accurate. Accurate measurements will assure you are always able to accurately estimate product costs and prices.

Chatbot Creator

With our Chatbot Creator, you don't need to hire a developer or invest
in expensive software to have a sweet chatbot on your website! You
can choose from the templates provided or build your own for your
specific needs.

Using this tool you can create a custom chatbot that can answer common
questions and handle the majority of the interactions with your clients. This
makes it easier for your client to be guided through the process, while they
are directly on your website without having to leave to go reach customer
support or sales. The chat messages can also be handled directly by one of
your employees - all within Green Space!

Call Back Calendar

Help keep your sales pipeline full and get organized with our Call Back
Calendar , which allows you to schedule when a call needs to be made and
record notes about your call.

The Call Back Calendar ensures that no client falls through the cracks by
helping you organize your call schedule.

Payment Processing / Mobile Card Reader

Securely accept all major credit cards, ACH, and automatic payments all within Green Space.
Our mobile card reader allows you to collect payment on the job site
Our mobile card reader is perfect for on-site jobs, such as landscaping, construction, cleaning and more. With our Mobile Card Reader, you can accept payments and tips on site. Plus, get detailed analytics of your collections so that you know exactly what's going on with your business finances!

Live Truck Tracking

Do you have a fleet of trucks that are constantly on the road? Is it important to
you to know exactly where they are and what they’re doing? Get real-time live
location tracking and management of your fleet with our Live Truck Tracking
solution. With Live Truck Tracking, you can track the locations of your drivers,
see their total miles for the day, and view the status of their jobs. You can even
receive alerts when oil changes or other maintenance items are due!

Reporting on Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Directly inspire improvement by understanding your business through reports. Make informed decisions based on your KPIs and utilize the reporting tool to generate over 20 different reports that can show you exactly where your business stands. Analyzing your key performance indicators can help you understand your business, and help you identify areas that need focus.

Custom Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) Widgets

Measuring the total number of clients sold this week? Tracking the amount of sales from a specific source? Whatever you can think of. It's all possible with KPI Widgets!

You and your employees will never miss a beat with custom widgets. KPI widgets give you the power to create your own beautiful and informative dashboard to display up-to-date and relevant information. Our widgets are perfect for any company that is looking for creative ways to display, view, and manage their internal performance.   

Stay Connected with Instant Messaging

Message users and clients all within Green Space. No need to pay for third party messaging apps anymore. Green Space's internal messenger allows you to organize your team and keep in touch with employees working in the field. Green Space makes it easy to create groups, send messages, attach files and stay connected with your employees and clients.

SMS Messaging with Clients

We know that clients love to text, so we've built an experience to let you send messages, estimates and other files over text. Send estimates or other files, keep in contact without interrupting workflow, and make sure that you are always on top of things.

Email Templates

Save time and maximize your success rates with email templates. Using the right template can help you create a great email that your clients will adore. Our pre-made email templates make writing emails quicker and easier. You can use them as they are or edit them to fit your needs. Improve response rates, increase new customer acquisition and boost existing client loyalty with email templates.

Email Tracking and Read Receipts

The right email delivery and read receipts can mean the difference between a
higher conversion rate and lost sales. With our software, you will have access
to reports on email results, including delivery receipts and read receipts.
Increase your sales by tracking which emails have been opened, so that you
can reply at just the right time—right after they’ve read your email message.

Easily Generate Estimates, Invoices, and Statements

Green Space is a simple, easy-to-use platform that helps you organize
and bill clients. We offer tools to help you generate estimates and
invoices, collect payments, and create professional statements. In your
letters, use your own custom logo, colors, fonts and more to create the
perfect look for your business.

Motivate Your Team with Sales and Production Boards

Sales and Production Boards are a great way to show you and your team how much each employee has produced for the week. They serve as a visual reminder to employees of what they can do, and also help boost motivation. Sales boards show how many sales have been made by each salesperson. Production boards show how many jobs have been done by each worker on any given day. Your teams will be much more likely to work harder and produce higher quality work with sales and production boards, showing them what they have achieved each week.

Set and Track Internal Goals with Custom Goal Thermometers

Custom Goal Thermometers are a simple, yet effective tool to track and encourage your team members' progress on their goals. Team members can create Goal Thermometers, set goals, track their progress and motivate others when they see the results. By providing your employees with a sense of accomplishment and pride in their work, you can help them feel more satisfied at work, which leads to increased productivity.

Track Employee Hours with Time Sheet Reports

Track employee hours and keep your staff happy with easy time sheet reporting. This web-based time clock makes it simple to track and report payroll data. Our hosted solution eliminates the hassle and expense of installing, maintaining and upgrading your own systems for payroll and attendance tracking.

Equip Your Teams in the Field All of the with the Green Space Mobile

With Green Space Mobile, you can access all of the great features of our
software on any Apple or Android mobile device! Enter job notes, view
details and locations for remaining jobs for the day, create estimates,
track expenses, and take payments on site with just a few taps.

Track Expenses and Create Projections with Quickbooks Integrations

Track your expenses, track progress on yearly projections, or create a budget with QuickBooks. Our plug-ins integrate seamlessly with your bookkeeping software to give you a complete picture of your business. QuickBooks integrations make it easy to keep expenses organized, payroll on track and create future projections so you can focus on growing your business with confidence.

Make More Phone Calls and Track Stats with the Easy-To-Use Integrated Preview Dialer

Dialing more leads on the phone is one of the most effective ways to generate more sales. Dialers allow you to make more phone calls at a much faster pace and increase your team’s productivity. Increase your sales with automated dialing with our automatic preview dialer. Track call outcome statistics with reports, complete with call duration and disposition results.

Stay on Top of Things with Automatically Generated Reports

Automatically generated reports keep you up to date with key performance
indicators and identify areas of improvement in your business. Save time spent
running the same report every week, and receive a custom PDF report emailed
directly to your inbox automatically as often as you want!

Dispatch Trucks to Complete Services with the Routing Tool

The Routing Tool is a simple and fast way to assign jobs to your drivers. View jobs on a map, select jobs to view the drive time and job prices. Then create the most efficient routes for your fleet. Use the Routing Tool to find the most efficient, cost-effective way to schedule your employee’s work day. Our routing tool is designed to help you create efficient routes, and saves you money on employee wages, fuel and vehicle wear-and-tear.
